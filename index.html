FreeUKGenealogy Donation Tool

Through Social Coders I found an opportunity to volunteer with FreeUKGenealogy who are in the process of migrating to WordPress and completely overhauling their existing website. I took on the project of designing a donation tool that they can use not only on the FreeUKGen site, but on all of their related sites (not moving to WordPress).

I created this tool with a combination of HTML, JavaScript, and CSS according to the designs provided to FreeUKGen by 48in48.
