FreeUKGenealogy Donation Tool

Through Social Coders I found FreeUKGenealogy who are currently migrating to WordPress and completely overhauling their current website. I took on the project of designing a donation tool that they can use not only on the FreeUKGen site, but on all of their related sites (not moving to WordPress).

I created this tool with a combination of HTML, JavaScript, and CSS according to the designs provided FreeUKGen by 48in48.
